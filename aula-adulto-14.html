Texto: Tom's Dream
Pesquise as palavras que não conhece
Tom wants to travel to Japan next year. He loves Japanese food and culture, and his dream is to see Mount Fuji and visit Tokyo Disneyland. However, he needs to save a lot of money because the trip is expensive. Tom works at a small company, so he tries to save part of his salary every month. He also needs to renew his passport and take English classes before traveling, because he wants to communicate better when he gets there. Sometimes Tom feels tired because he studies and works hard, but he keeps going because he really wants to make his dream come true.
