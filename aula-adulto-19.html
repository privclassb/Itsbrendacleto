Movie Review: Inside Out 2
Pesquise as palavras que não conhece
Inside Out 2 is a new animated movie by Disney and Pixar. It is about a girl named Riley and her emotions — Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear and Anxiety. The movie is very funny and emotional, but sometimes sad. It is also interesting because it shows how people feel when they grow up. The animation is beautiful, and the story is easy to understand. I think the movie is great for families and children. It is a bit long, but not boring. I prefer to watch animated movies because they make me feel happy.
